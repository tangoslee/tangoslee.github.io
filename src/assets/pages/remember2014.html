Remember Project

Jun 2014, Cyworld co.LTD.

Introduction & Goal:

This project goal was focusing on different curation for the Cyworld Photo Album. Cyworld Photo Album was the largest social media in Korea. A lot of Korean had managed their photos using that service for over ten years before the Facebook appear. We would have liked that people could look back their good memory through our service.

Role:

I was responsible for developing API server for Android and iOS app. There were over 15 types of different message formats in the legacy system, so I redefined new message format and making a live transcoder.

Result:

Even though we could not have come back to the mainstream of the social media, we made people remember a good time.
  • Back-end: PHP5, CodeIgniter, Memcached, Linux, SQLServer
