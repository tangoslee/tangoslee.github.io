Post Publisher

2015, Cyworld Inc.

Introduction & Goal:

When a user writes a post once, it will publish major social media services automatically.

Role:

Designed the structure and implemented both a publishing system and oauth2 token store.

Result:

Users who wantted to post similar contents to major media can publish their posts to where they wanted.
  • Front-end: Javascript, jQuery
  • Back-end: PHP, RESTful API, MySQL, Memcached, MQ
